This site uses Google Analytics. It's something I don't like doing, but the curiosity of who's viewing this site from where gets to me.

I probably won't get rid of analytics ever because of this, but as an act of good faith, I'll recommend two adblockers that'll stop me from collecting your data. If you're using Chrome (or a better, Chromium-based like The Browser Company’s Arc [the one I use]) check out uBlockOrigin. It's good, it's free, and best of all, it's open source. Gotta love it. If you're using Safari or are reading this on an Apple product, make the upgrade to 1Blocker. It costs $3/mo., but it's a great experience, constantly gets updated and can block iPhone apps from tracking you as well. Not getting paid by any of these people, just a fan of their products.

No other tracking is taking place, feel free to check out the site’s source code.
